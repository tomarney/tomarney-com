I am a palaeoceanographer studying for a PhD at the [National Oceanography Centre](https://www.noc.ac.uk), [University of Southampton](https://www.southampton.ac.uk/oes), and the [British Antarctic Survey](https://www.bas.ac.uk).

My project concerns the early Pleistocene palaeoceanography of the West Antarctic margin, and at the moment I’m analysing a carbonate-rich sedimentary record from the Byrd Seamounts in the Amundsen Sea.
